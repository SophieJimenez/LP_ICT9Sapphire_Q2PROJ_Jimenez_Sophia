Dragon Maki 101

Ingredients:

For Sticky Rice: For Sushi Topping:
2 Cups Sushi Rice Nori (Seaweed Sheets)
Â½ Cup Water Masago (Orange Fish Roe)
4 tbsp Rice Vinegar Thinly Sliced Avocado
3 tbsp Sugar Thinly Sliced Cucumber
1 tsp Salt Grilled Eel (Unagi) or Shrimp Tempura
1 tbsp Sesame Seeds (Optional) Spicy Mayo or Eel Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook sushi rice with water, then season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Spread rice on nori, add masago, avocado, cucumber, and eel or shrimp tempura.
  3. Roll tightly using a sushi mat and slice into pieces.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and drizzle with spicy mayo or eel sauce.
