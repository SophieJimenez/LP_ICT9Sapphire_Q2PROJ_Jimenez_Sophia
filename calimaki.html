California Maki 101

Ingredients:

>
For Sticky Rice: For Sushi Topping:
2 Cups Japanese Rice Nori (Seaweed)
½ Cup Cold Water Masago (Orange Fish Eggs)
4 tbsp Rice Vinegar Sliced Kani (Crab Sticks)
3 tbsp Sugar Sliced Cucumber
1 tsp Salt Sliced Mango

Steps:

  1. Cook 2 cups rice with ½ cup water.
  2. Mix 4 tbsp rice vinegar, 3 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp salt; massage into cooked rice.
  3. Layer Nori, rice, masago (fish eggs), mango, kani, and cucumber.
  4. Roll with a sushi mat, slice, and enjoy!
