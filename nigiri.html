Salmon Nigiri 101

Ingredients:

For Salmon Nigiri:
2 Cups Japanese Rice
2.5 Cups of Water
4 tbsp Rice Vinegar
3 tbsp Sugar
1 tsp Salt
Raw Salmon

Steps:

  1. Cook 2 cups of rice with 2.5 cups of water (adjust for the right texture).
  2. Mix 4 tbsp rice vinegar, 3 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp salt; stir into the cooked rice.
  3. Shape the rice into small, oval mounds with your hands.
  4. Slice raw salmon into thin strips.
  5. Top each rice mound with a piece of salmon and enjoy!
